Totems are further broken up into female and male.
For example for Mbizi, females are called Madhuve whilst
the males are Dhuve or Mazvimbakupa.

People of the same totem are believed to be related through
a common ancestor therefore are not allowed to marry. People
believe it is a taboo to eat your own totem .

Totem praise

People of different totems use different praise names for
example Mhofu and eland use Mutenhesanwa , Mwendamberi and Chihera among
others.Gumbo uses Madyirapanze.
