Data presentation techniques

The researchers use data presentation analysis because information presented in graphs is easy to interpret and understand.
It also saves time and cost.
Such information covers a wide range of users` needs.

There are two methods of data analysis which quantitative and qualitative analysis.

Qualitative ARE MERE FACTS SENTIMENTS from findings . There are used in explaining and summarizing the findings losing the original data.

Tables,graphs and charts are quantitative techniques used to present data.

Advantages of using charts , tables e.t.c

  • Makes information clear.
  • Allows users to see the overall picture of findings and the results are easy to understand.
  • The overall pattern can be seen and also results are shown in compact form
  • The technique also makes comparisons easy between different sets of data and numbers and/or qualitative measures are used to support the graphs.
